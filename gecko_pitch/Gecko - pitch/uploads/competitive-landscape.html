Top 3 Closest Competitors

Daisy Pay
Solana stablecoins · TikTok / Instagram · $3.9M seed 2025
Live Med Threat
What:Brands pay micro-creators to "boost" brand content via social engagement (comments, likes, shares). Moving to Solana stablecoin payment rails. $3.9M seed 2025.
Traction:2,000 creators, 50 brand clients, $1M revenue in 7 months, $300K/quarter paid to creators.
Strength:Real Web2→Web3 bridge, genuine brand revenue, Solana payment rails, growing creator network.
Weakness:Micro-engagement ≠ campaigns. No vault, no cliff, no yield, no milestone auto-release. Brand can cancel. Creator can ghost. Centralized payment despite Solana rail.
Gecko edge:Daisy is a payment router. Gecko is an enforcement protocol. Daisy's brand can still DM the creator on Telegram to cancel — Gecko's program prevents it.
Lumanu
Off-chain SaaS · Global ACH/SEPA/wire · Enterprise influencer payments
Live High Threat
What:Enterprise B2B SaaS for influencer payments. Centralized escrow, milestone-based payout triggers, W-9/1099 compliance, global rails (ACH, SEPA, Faster Payments). Single vendor of record for brands paying 50–500 creators.
Traction:Dominant in Web2 influencer payment space. Used by major agencies and DTC brands. Real revenue, real enterprise clients.
Strength:Established brand trust, compliance solved (W-9/AML/OFAC), global payment rails, milestone triggers already familiar to brand teams.
Weakness:Any brand can log in and reverse a payment. "Escrow" is a Lumanu bank account — they are the intermediary. No on-chain verifiability. No yield on locked capital. US-centric compliance breaks for LATAM/Web3 native creators. Brand can dispute and Lumanu adjudicates.
Gecko edge:Lumanu IS Lever.io at enterprise scale. Gecko's cliff makes reversal impossible by design — not by policy. For web3-native brands, using Lumanu is an embarrassing web2 detour. Yield on locked capital is pure upside Lumanu can never offer.
Influur
Ethereum ecosystem · Smart contract payments · 30K+ crypto-native creators
Live Med Threat
What:Web3-native influencer marketplace with smart contract payment disbursement. Database of 30,000+ NFT artists, DAO operators, and crypto-native KOLs. Built for crypto projects promoting to crypto-native audiences.
Traction:30K+ creator profiles, real Web3 brand clients, on-chain payment history. ETH-focused.
Strength:Largest Web3-native creator database, on-chain payment transparency, smart contracts prevent basic disputes.
Weakness:Marketplace model (discovery), not protocol model (enforcement). No cliff enforcement, no yield, no oracle scoring, no milestone auto-release. ETH ecosystem — no Solana presence. Creator vetting is centralized.
Gecko edge:Influur connects brands and creators. Gecko enforces what they agree to. No overlap on discovery — full overlap on the settlement layer. Gecko on Solana = 10× cheaper, 10× faster per tx than Influur on ETH.

Differentiation Recommendation

Gecko's Unfair Advantage: The "Brand Can't Rug" Guarantee

No competitor combines all four: on-chain budget cliff-locking + guaranteed floor yield + oracle-scored delivery + milestone auto-release. Lumanu has milestone triggers but a human can reverse them. Daisy has Solana rails but no enforcement. Influur has on-chain payments but no yield or cliff. Gecko owns the intersection.

  • Lead with the creator guarantee, not the tech. "Your campaign payment is locked on-chain. The brand cannot cancel before the cliff." This is the headline.
  • Lumanu is the proof the market exists. Enterprise brands already pay for escrow-like infrastructure. Gecko is Lumanu but irreversible — and the locked capital earns yield while waiting.
  • LATAM first. Trexx, LOUD, Furia = fastest distribution. LATAM creators have the worst experience with international brand payment delays — Gecko's USDC cliff-lock is a direct solution.
  • Yield is a bonus, not the pitch. Kamino integration is a differentiator from Lever.io/Lumanu but don't lead with it — "yield" sounds DeFi-y to Web2 creators. Say "your locked budget earns interest while the campaign runs."
  • Never say SocialFi. The 2023–2024 SocialFi wave is toxic. Position as "creator payment infrastructure" or "brand campaign protocol."
  • Squad campaigns are unique. No competitor has a native multi-creator allocation primitive with on-chain enforcement. Lead with this for agencies running team-based esports campaigns.
  • Protocol fee is the business model. 0.5% on every vault regardless of who built the UI. SDK partners bring distribution — Gecko captures economics at settlement layer.
